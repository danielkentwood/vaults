# Least Squares
Least squares is a form of [[Linear Models | linear model]].

Given a vector of inputs $X^{T}=(X_{1},X_{2},...,X_{p})$, we want to predict $Y$ with the following:
$$
\hat{Y}=\hat{\beta}_{0} + \sum_{j=1}^{p}X_{j}\hat{\beta}_{j}
$$
