This is a type of [[exploratory data analysis]] that classifies/groups individuals on the basis of how an intervention (or a personalization of the intervention) is expected to affect them.

When archetype discovery is motivated by a behavior change campaign, it is helpful to think about *barriers to behavior change*. What are the unique challenges and friction points that surface in our attempts to change behavior, and do these challenges appear more often in certain subsets of people?
